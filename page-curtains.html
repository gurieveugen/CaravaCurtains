Our Curtains

At Caravan Curtains we can make curtains for your caravan, 4WD, bus, boat, truck, Combi van, and anything else that requires small curtains.

The curtains in our standard range are made from 100% polyester with acrylic-coated backing. We use 1 Pass lightweight fabric, which is suitable for small windows and pleated pelmets.

For extra privacy, you can choose to have full block-out (an additional charge will apply).

Choose your caravan curtains from our great range:

Standard curtains,

60mm top hem – suitable for hanging on rods or stretch wire
30mm bottom hem – allows for stretch wire if the curtain need to be fixed at the bottom

'Jayco-style' curtains,

Suitable for use with rods
Comes with tape headings, which can also be applied to the bottom
Great alternative if there's no pelmet to cover top of curtain – when gathered, curtains form a pleated look

Pleated Tops,

Small pleats are sewn into top of curtain
Includes hooks which are inserted into pleats and can be used with any type of rings or tracks (hooks are fitted and included in the price)
Can also be used as room divider curtains

We can make your curtains in any size, so if you have an unusual style of fitting, please contact us and we'll be happy to advise you on the best style of curtains and fixtures for your caravan.
